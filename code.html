About Traspick


the formation of this application can help a lot of people who have problem with their household trash management, also the efficiency in terms of time. Customer just order when they want their trash to be picked up by the garbage collector.


Background: Indonesia generates approximately 7.8 million tons of plastic waste annually. 4.9 million tons of plastic waste is mismanaged—e.g. uncollected, disposed of in open dumpsites or leaked from improperly managed landfills. There’s no recycling for the Trash and the amount of recycling done is only 14.58% of its waste, while 34.60% is managed – as in dumped in landfills or incinerated – thereby bringing the national waste management to 49.18%. People usually burn the trash instead of recycling it to reduce it. It cause air pollution. People also often litter, either out of habit or not knowing where the trash can is. People also find it difficult to throw their household waste into the final landfill.
